Club Achievements

Celebrating the outstanding accomplishments of our student clubs and their members.

Competition

National Robotics Championship — 1st Place

The UniClubs Robotics Society took home first place at the 2024 National Inter-University Robotics Challenge, beating out 34 competing teams. Their autonomous navigation robot, Axiom-7, completed the obstacle course a full 12 seconds faster than the nearest competitor.

November 2024
🎤
Event Organisation

TEDx Campus Talk — 800+ Attendees

The Debate & Public Speaking Club successfully organised and ran a fully licensed TEDx event on campus, hosting 10 student speakers across topics ranging from climate innovation to mental health advocacy. The event attracted over 800 attendees and was livestreamed to an audience of 3,000.

March 2025
🌍
Member Accomplishment

UN Youth Forum Delegate — Sofia Merani

Sofia Merani, Vice-President of the International Affairs Society, was selected as one of only 50 delegates worldwide to represent her country at the United Nations Youth Climate Forum in Geneva. She presented her policy paper on sustainable urban transport, which was commended by the panel.

July 2025
