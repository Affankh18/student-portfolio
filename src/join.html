How to Join a Student Club

Ready to dive in? Follow these simple steps to become an official club member!

Step-by-Step Registration

  1. Explore: Browse the 'Club Categories' page to find a club that matches your interests.
  2. Register: Fill out the online membership form available on the university student portal.
  3. Attend Orientation: Join the introductory session during the first week of the semester to meet the executive body.

Meeting Schedules & Guidelines

  • Schedules: Most clubs meet bi-weekly on Wednesdays or Fridays between 3:00 PM and 5:00 PM.
  • Commitment: Active members are expected to attend at least 70% of general body meetings.
  • Code of Conduct: All members must adhere to the university's values of respect, inclusivity, and collaboration.
